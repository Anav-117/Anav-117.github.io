Physically Based Rendering

- About Project

Implementation of the Physically Based Render (PBR) Pipeline with Image Based Lighting (IBL) in OpenGL. The PBR pipeline is widely used for rendering physically accurate material, particularly reflections and other material properties.

The renderer can sample the environment around a model and generate accurate and high definition reflections on the surface of the model. Renders of materials with varying properties from polished metals to porcelain can be effectively rendered.
